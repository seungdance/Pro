About Me

Hi, I'm Seung Hwan Lee

A freelance artist from South Korea, currently based in Mannheim, Germany. My creative background comes from contemporary dance and performance, and now I'm expanding my journey into the world of web development.

I'm passionate about crafting not only physical movement, but also meaningful, intuitive digital experiences. As I begin this new chapter as a web developer, I bring a unique perspective that combines structure, emotion, and aesthetics both on stage and on screen.
